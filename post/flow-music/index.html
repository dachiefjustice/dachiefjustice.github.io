Flow Music

 Posted on March 31, 2018  |  1 minutes (153 words)

What helps you get “in the zone” or into a “flow state”? Music does! This post links to some of my favorite music for concentration, maybe you’ll find something you like.

Thanks to a musical upbringing, my tastes are all over the place. Quoting the great Duke Ellington:

There are two kinds of music. Good music, and the other kind.

Genre doesn’t matter, so long as the music’s good! I find lyrics distracting when I’m focusing, so this post is mostly instrumentals.

Jazz

  • Mockroot from Tigran Hamasyan
  • Seven Seas from Avishai Cohen (the whole album, especially the title track)
  • Breakthrough from Eldar Djangirov, especially Point of View Redux

Electronic

  • Level Music Best from Maxo
  • SNESQUE from Zackery Wilson
  • Triple J mix from Madeon

Other

  • Joy from Shakti
  • Joy of Motion from Animals as Leaders
  • U.K. from U.K.
  • Bach Cello Suites performed by Yo-Yo Ma

Dig the music? Support the artists, buy their stuff!
