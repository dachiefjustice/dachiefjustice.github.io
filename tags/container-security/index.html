Container Security Part I

Background

Posted on March 26, 2018

Containerization has exploded in popularity across the tech scene in recent years, driven by Docker and container orchestration platforms like Kubernetes. Containers are a natural fit for microservice-based applications and for CI/CD² pipeline tasks. They power event-driven function-as-a-service (“serverless”) platforms like AWS Lambda and Apache OpenWhisk. Containerization offers substantial benefits, much discussed across the tech world. Avoiding vendor lock-in, environment consistency, application portability, rapid deployment/scaling, deployment density – a range of business and technical benefits is likely to drive continued adoption.
